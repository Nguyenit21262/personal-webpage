Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization with an all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission is to bring internationally renowned, thought-provoking speakers to educate, and entertain our audience! As one of our members said:

"Each year I give a ticket package to each of our members. I think of it as the gift of knowledge... and it’s priceless."

Speaker of the Month

Fossil Threads in the Web of Life

February

Scott Sampson

What’s 75 million years old and
brand spanking new? A teenage
Uatchceratops! Come to the
Saroyan, armed with your best
dinosaur roar, when Scott
Sampson, Research Curator at the
Utah Museum of Natural History,
steps to the podium. Sampson’s
research has focused on the
ecology and evolution of late
Cretaceous dinosaurs and he has
conducted fieldwork in a number of
countries in Africa.
